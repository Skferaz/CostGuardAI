Connect AWS Account

Step 1 — Create IAM Role in your AWS account

Run this in your AWS account's CloudShell or CLI:

aws iam create-role --role-name CostGuardReadRole --assume-role-policy-document '{"Version":"2012-10-17","Statement":[{"Effect":"Allow","Principal":{"AWS":"arn:aws:iam::651592873649:role/costguard-lambda-role"},"Action":"sts:AssumeRole"}]}' aws iam put-role-policy --role-name CostGuardReadRole --policy-name CostExplorerRead --policy-document '{"Version":"2012-10-17","Statement":[{"Effect":"Allow","Action":["ce:GetCostAndUsage","ce:GetCostForecast"],"Resource":"*"}]}'

Step 2 — Get your Role ARN

aws iam get-role --role-name CostGuardReadRole --query 'Role.Arn' --output text

It looks like: arn:aws:iam::123456789012:role/CostGuardReadRole

Step 3 — Register your account

Paste your Role ARN below and we'll start monitoring your costs.
